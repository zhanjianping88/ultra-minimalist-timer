What to Include

If you contact support, please include your device model, iOS version, app version, and a short description of the issue.

Common Issues

  • Make sure notification permission is enabled if you want lock screen alerts.
  • Check that Sounds are enabled in both the app and iPhone notification settings.
  • On iPhone, Focus modes and system mute behavior may affect how alerts are delivered.
