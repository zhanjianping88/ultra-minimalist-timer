Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 31, 2026

Ultra Minimalist Timer is designed to work locally on your device. We do not require account creation, and we do not sell your personal information.

Information We Collect

Ultra Minimalist Timer does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or payment information.

Local Data Storage

The app stores your settings locally on your device using system storage such as UserDefaults. This may include your default timer duration, sound preference, haptics preference, and selected alarm sound. This information stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.

Notifications

If you grant notification permission, the app uses local notifications only to alert you when a timer ends. Notification scheduling happens on your device.

Third-Party Services

Ultra Minimalist Timer does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or rent personal data because the app does not collect personal data from you.

Children’s Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page.
